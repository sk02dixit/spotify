    ABOUT US





    Jalan Group is a Varanasi (U.P.) based business house engaged in textile trading through its wholesale and retail enterprises. It was set up with the pioneering effort of Late Sri Deendayal Jalan in 1974. The organization is inspired by the principles and ideologies of its founder Late. Sri Deendayal Jalan, who believes in providing the best products and services to customers at the most reasonable price. The best use of technology, human resource and business acumen, has brought the Jalan Group to be one of the leading corporate of this region, and on its way to becoming the National Textile Conglomerate. Jalan Group, the leader in textile trading is also having two retail outllets-“Jalan’s” at Varanasi in addition to its whole setup Jalan Synthetics.. Started as one store enterprise to support the whole sale trading and to directly provide cloths to consumers at lower price in 1999,has become today the Biggest cloth store with highest sale in North India. These shops offer affordable cloth&clothings at lowest possible rates to suit every segment of the society. From one shop to multiple, from one floor to five floors ,it has grown up very fast catering all price ranges. The cost benefit that is derived from the large direct &experienced wholesale purchase with strong office network throughout India is passed on to customer , therefore the slogan of business is ‘Sasta Bhi Acchha Bhi‘. A good shopping environment ,state of art technoplogy ,a seamless supply chain infrastructure,a host of unique value- added services and above all ,customer from the heart makes these stores Paradise for the thousands of the visitors daily. With an objective of “Offering the Best Product at most Economical price“,Jalan’s never look back ,It has broken down national news many times with its offers, speciality ,celebrity buying etc.
